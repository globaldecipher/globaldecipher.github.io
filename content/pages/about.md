## Mission

The Global Decipher is an independent digital research publication providing structured coverage of terrorism, militant organisations, extremist media ecosystems, and security risk trends.

TGD publishes for researchers, journalists, policy analysts, students, and readers who need concise public-source context without propaganda amplification or operational detail.

## What we publish

- Timely public-source news briefings and analytical commentary.
- Monitoring notes that distinguish initial claims from confirmed information.
- Monthly and thematic research reports with downloadable source editions.
- Profiles of militant actors and organisations, plus interactive incident and network tools.

## Editorial standards

TGD separates reporting, claims, and analytical assessment. Read the [methodology](/methodology/), [corrections policy](/corrections-policy/), and [privacy policy](/privacy-policy/) for the standards behind the publication.
