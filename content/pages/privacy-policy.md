## Overview

This static website does not collect account registrations, passwords, payment details, or private user profiles.

## Contact messages

If you email TGD, your email address and message content may be used only to respond to your inquiry.

## Analytics and advertising

Version 1 does not include analytics or advertising scripts. If analytics or AdSense are added later, this policy should be updated before those tools go live.

## Third-party links

The website links to X, WhatsApp, Substack, and other public websites. Those platforms have their own privacy policies.
