## Overview

This static website does not collect account registrations, passwords, payment details, or private user profiles. It stores a light-or-dark theme preference in your browser when you use the theme switcher.

## Contact messages

If you email TGD, your email address and message content may be used only to respond to your inquiry.

## Fonts, analytics, and advertising

The site loads typefaces from Google Fonts, so your browser may connect to Google's font servers when a page opens. The site does not currently include analytics or advertising scripts. This policy should be updated before either is added.

## Third-party links

The website links to X, WhatsApp, Substack, and other public websites. Those platforms have their own privacy policies.
