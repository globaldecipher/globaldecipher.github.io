## Corrections

TGD will correct factual errors when identified. Corrections should explain what changed and, where appropriate, why the earlier version was incomplete or inaccurate.

## Updates

Security reporting develops over time. When new public information changes an assessment, TGD may update the relevant page and note the update date.

## Reader requests

Readers can send [correction requests](mailto:globaldecipher@gmail.com?subject=TGD%20correction%20request) with the page title, disputed claim, and supporting public source.
