## Status

Identified in UN sanctions material and U.S. Rewards for Justice material as al-Shabaab's leader. Rewards for Justice has offered up to $10 million for information.

## Identification

- **Name:** Ahmed Diriye
- **Alias:** Abu Ubaidah
- **Organisation:** Al-Shabaab
- **Role:** Emir after Godane's death in September 2014

## Background

Diriye's core profile is continuity under a low-visibility style. The public record is much thinner on childhood and personality than it is for his predecessor Ahmed Abdi Godane.

The UN sanctions narrative says he became emir of al-Shabaab after Godane's death and bears command responsibility for the group's operations.

## Significance

One important succession detail is that Abu Ubaidah was associated with the faction that helped enforce Godane's 2013 purge of dissenters. That places him not as a placeholder successor but as a participant in the machinery that preserved al-Shabaab cohesion after leader decapitation.
