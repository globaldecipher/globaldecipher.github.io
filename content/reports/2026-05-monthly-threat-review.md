## Report overview

This sample report format is designed for monthly publication. It gives TGD a repeatable product that readers can expect, share, and cite.

## Sections

- Key developments.
- Regional incident summary.
- Propaganda and claim patterns.
- Confidence notes.
- Corrections and updates from prior reporting.

## Research value

Monthly rhythm helps separate noise from trend. It also gives institutions a clearer view of what changed, what stayed consistent, and what requires closer monitoring.
