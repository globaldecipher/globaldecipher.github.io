## Premium preview

This report product is designed for institutions that need structured district-level analysis. The full report would include a transparent methodology, source confidence ratings, and a non-operational risk summary.

## Public summary

District-level assessments should avoid alarmism. The useful question is not simply whether an area is risky, but what type of public-source signal is changing and how reliable that signal is.
