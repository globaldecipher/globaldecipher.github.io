How we work

Methodology

TGD uses public-source discipline, confidence labels, and a clear distinction between claims, confirmations, and assessments.

Core principles

TGD uses public sources and avoids publishing operational details that could endanger people or assist violent actors. We separate claims from confirmations and label uncertainty where it remains.

Source handling

  • Public reporting, official statements, and verified institutional material are prioritized.
  • Claims by militant actors are treated as claims, not confirmation.
  • Sensitive propaganda material is not embedded, linked, or reproduced for attention.
  • Corrections are published when new information changes a prior assessment.

Confidence language

TGD uses clear language to distinguish verified facts, public claims, and analytical assessments. This helps readers understand what is known, what is likely, and what remains uncertain.
