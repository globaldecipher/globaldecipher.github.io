Profile Framework: Individual Actor Research Template

A safe framework for individual profiles that emphasizes verification, relevance, and public-interest limits.

Apr 18, 2026 TGD Research Unit South Asia Research sensitive

Profile purpose

Individual profiles require special caution. TGD should only publish them when there is a clear public-interest reason and enough source confidence to avoid misidentification.

Recommended fields

  • Publicly reported role.
  • Group affiliation, if verified.
  • Geographic relevance.
  • Timeline of public reporting.
  • Source confidence and caveats.
  • Corrections history.

Safety note

Profiles should avoid private personal information, family details, unverified accusations, and anything that could create avoidable harm.
