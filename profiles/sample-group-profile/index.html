Profile Framework: Militant Organization Research Template

A safe public template for building terrorist organization profiles without amplifying propaganda or tactical material.

Apr 20, 2026 TGD Research Unit Global Free

Profile purpose

TGD profiles are designed to help readers understand actors, not to promote them. Each profile should focus on history, ideology, public claims, areas of activity, leadership changes, and source confidence.

Recommended structure

  • Overview.
  • Origins and evolution.
  • Leadership and factional structure.
  • Ideological framing.
  • Areas of activity.
  • Notable public claims and verified incidents.
  • Source reliability notes.

Editorial boundary

Profiles should not include recruitment material, tactical guidance, graphic media, or direct links to extremist propaganda.
